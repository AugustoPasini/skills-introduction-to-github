What We Offer

Full-service care,
every season.

From spring cleanup to fall leaf removal, we handle every aspect of your lawn and property. Click any service below to learn exactly what we do.

01

Lawn Mowing & Edging

Clean cuts, crisp edges, and a professional finish every visit. We mow at the right height for your grass type and always edge along driveways, walkways, and beds.

We cut your lawn to the ideal height for your specific grass type and time of season — never too short to cause stress, never too long to look unkempt. Every visit includes clean edging along all driveways, sidewalks, curb lines, and garden bed borders for crisp, defined lines. Clippings are blown off all hard surfaces before we leave. Available as a one-time cut or on a regular weekly or bi-weekly schedule.
02

Hedge & Shrub Trimming

Precision shaping and trimming to keep your hedges, topiaries, and shrubs looking sculptured. We handle everything from boxwoods to privacy hedges.

From compact boxwood borders to tall arborvitae privacy hedges, we shape and maintain any type of shrub or ornamental hedge. We use commercial-grade shears and loppers to achieve clean, even lines and remove dead or overgrown growth throughout the plant. All trimmings and debris are collected and hauled away at the end of every job, leaving your property looking sharp.
03

Mulching & Top Dressing

Fresh mulch installation to suppress weeds, retain moisture, and give your beds a polished, finished look. We haul, spread, and clean up everything.

We source, deliver, and install fresh mulch — shredded hardwood, cedar, or color-enhanced options available depending on your preference. Applied at the correct 2–3 inch depth, mulch suppresses weed growth, retains soil moisture, and insulates roots through temperature extremes. Service includes edging all bed borders and clearing out any old compacted material before the new mulch goes down.
04

Leaf Cleanup & Fall Care

Complete fall leaf removal, haul-away, and end-of-season cleanup. We clear your lawn, beds, and gutters so your yard is ready for whatever winter brings.

Our fall cleanup covers your full property — lawns are blown and raked clean, all garden beds are cleared of fallen leaves and debris, and walkways are tidied up. We typically schedule multiple visits through the season as leaves continue to fall. All material is bagged and hauled off the property. Gutter clearing is available as an add-on service at the time of booking.
